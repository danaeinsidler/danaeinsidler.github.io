First Things First

Based on the First Things First manifestos, this series of work is meant to express my opinion towards design. People should be allowed to push boundaries in all forms of design. However, designers also need to be aware of the responsibility that comes with communicating ideas. While sexuality should not be shunned, there is certainly a fine line between sexuality and sexism.
