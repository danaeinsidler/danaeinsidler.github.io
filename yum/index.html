Lemon Dome

Ingredients
  • 1 box yellow cake mix
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • Grated zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 1/3 cups frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
  • confectioner's sugar
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 325ºF. Grease and flour a 2-quart ovenproof metal mixing bowl.
  2. In another medium bowl, combine the cake mix, eggs, oil, and lemon zest, along with the amount of water called for by the package directions. Beat until well combined. Pour the batter into the prepared bowl.
  3. Place in oven and bake for about 50 minutes. Let cool for 20 minutes before unmolding onto a rack.
  4. Wash and dry the bowl you baked the cake in. Line it with plastic wrap and place the cake back in the bowl (support the bottom so the dome doesn’t break). Using a skewer, liberally pierce the cake, poking through to the bowl.
  5. Pour the lemonade concentrate over the top. Let the liquid soak in for 15 minutes before unmolding the cake upside-down onto a platter.
  6. Just before serving, dust lightly with the confectioners’ sugar.
